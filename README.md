# Customer Churn Analysis & Future Churn Prediction

## About the Project

This project was built to understand why customers leave a company and to identify customers who are likely to churn in the future. I used Python for prediction and Power BI to build interactive dashboards that help visualize customer behavior, churn patterns and business impact.

The project includes both historical churn analysis and future churn prediction so that business users can compare past trends with future risks.

---

## Project Summary

- Analyzed **6.42K customer records**
- Found **1.73K historical churned customers**
- Predicted **476 future churners**
- Predicted churn rate of **10.16%**
- Total revenue analyzed: **₹19.47M**
- Revenue at risk: **₹883.55K**

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Customer Churn Analysis

This page focuses on historical customer churn.

It includes:
- Total Customers
- Total Churn
- Churn Rate
- Total Revenue
- New Customers
- Churn by Gender
- Churn by Age Group
- Churn by Contract
- Churn by Category
- Service Analysis
- Top States by Churn Rate

---

### Future Churn Prediction

This dashboard shows customers who are likely to churn based on the prediction model.

It includes:
- Active Customers
- Predicted Churners
- Predicted Churn Rate
- Revenue at Risk
- High Risk Customers
- Predicted Churners by Gender
- Predicted Churners by Age Group
- Predicted Churners by Contract
- Predicted Churners by Risk Level
- Top States with Predicted Churners

---

### Predicted Churners Details

This page provides customer-level information for all predicted churners.

The report can be filtered using different slicers to quickly find specific customers and analyze their details.
